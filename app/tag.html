关于FT中文网

FT中文网 (http://www.FTChinese.com) 是英国《金融时报》集团旗下唯一的中文商业财经网站，旨在为中国商业菁英和决策者们提供每日不可或缺的商业财经资讯、深度分析以及评论。凭借英国《金融时报》遍布全球的丰富资源，FT中文网深入分析对中国经济和全球商业具有影响力的重大事件，并揭示事件的来龙去脉，以真正富有国际视野的权威内容成为中国高级管理人员“必读”的商业财经资讯网站。FT中文网目前拥有逾300万注册用户，移动产品已覆盖iOS、Android和Windows系统。2018年4月，FT中文网正式推出付费订阅业务，为专属会员提供精选资讯与个性化服务。

FTChinese is the Financial Times' Chinese language website, providing unrivalled news and information to China's top business executives and decision makers. The FT’s international team of journalists has access to senior corporate executives and politicians both in China and around the world, making it a must read for China's business leaders. FTChinese has over 3 million registered users and is also available via iOS, Android and Windows platforms. In April 2018, FTChinese launched its paid-for subscription service.

关于 DealStreetAsia

With a team of journalists based in nine markets, DealStreetAsia provides readers with exclusive insights, industry-leading analysis, and research reports based on proprietary data. Its content includes exclusive interviews with top global leaders, enabling readers to access regional dynamics and valuable business intelligence.
